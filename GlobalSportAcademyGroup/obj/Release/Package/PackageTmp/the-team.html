    Todd Ford - Hockey Coach - Goaltender Specialist

    Who Am I

    Todd was born and raised in Calgary, AB playing hockey locally as a goalie. In 2000 Todd won himself a spot on the WHL Swift Current Broncos where he would further garner his goalie skills and eventually go on to continue his WHL career with the Prince George Cougars and Vancouver Giants. Todd was fortunate to be given the opportunity to showcase his skills in the 2002 CHL TOP Prospects game on route to one of his lifetime hockey highlights – being drafted that same year to the Toronto Maple Leafs as part of the 2002 NHL Draft. Since that time Todd played over 8 years of professional hockey with various organizations throughout the USA primarily in the AHL and ECHL.

      • Named to two ECHL All-Star teams, named a first team ECHL All-Star (2009-2010),
      • Named the ECHL Goaltender of the year (2009-2010)
      • Spent the later part of his North American Hockey Career playing as a member of the renowned AHL team the Hershey Bears and ultimately found himself sporting an NHL Washington Capitals Jersey for a short stint.
      • Upon the conclusion of his North American Hockey Career, Todd ventured to Germany and enjoyed a season abroad as a starting goalie for a prominent team.
      • While playing professionally, Todd has also been a goalie coach at all levels of the game for almost a dozen years.
      • Currently Todd works as an independent consultant for the Calgary Flames Hockey School, is the goalie coach for both the Men’s and Women’s varsity Mount Royal University Hockey Teams and is the goalie coach to the Junior A Okotoks Oilers
      • Additionally, Todd enjoys working independently with organizations looking to develop their goaltending talent.
